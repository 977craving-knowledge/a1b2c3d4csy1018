A Short Biography

My name is Sandesh Gautam. My parents are Mr.Kamal Raj Gautam and Mrs.Tara Devi Gautam. I was born in September in 1999. My home town is Urlabari, Morang but at present I am a resident of Bouddha, Jorpati. I am currently an undergraduate of NAMI College.

I live a very simple life in terms of entertainment with friends and family. But when it comes to knowledge, innovation and creation, these are adventures for me. Honestly I was into Mechanical Engineering not so long ago. But once I heard about this field of study (esp. Networking) and when I knew about the various day-to-day discovery going on in this field, I knew right there and then that this is what I was meant to study. And here I am studying Computing at one of Nepal's top IT institutes.

I consider myself to be a perfectionist and visionary and I am keen on making my nation more developed on the field of technology. And hopefully if all goes well, I will make my vision come true.
