A Short Biography

Hey! You probably know about me otherwise you wouldn't be on this site. Anyway, my name is Sandesh Gautam. My parents are Mr.Kamal Raj Gautam and Mrs.Tara Devi Gautam. I was born in 1999 A.D. My home town is Urlabari, Morang. I'm currently an undergraduate of NAMI College.

I live a very simple life in terms of entertainment with friends and family. But when it comes to knowledge, innovation and creation, these are adventures for me. Honestly I was into Mechanical Engineering not so long ago. But once I heard about this field of study (esp. Networking) and when I knew about the various day-to-day discovery going on in this field, I knew right there and then that this is what I was meant to study. And here I am studying Computing at one of Nepal's top IT institute.

I consider myself to be a perfectionist and visionary and I am keen on making my nation more developed on the field of technology. And hopefully if all goes well, I will make my vision come true.
